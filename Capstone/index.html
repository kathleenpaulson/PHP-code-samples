About Us

Pages is a small independent bookstore and cafe with a cozy feeling that encourages you to put up your feet and stay a while. We are furnished with comfortable sofas and chairs and all the free coffee you can drink. We sell new and used books, magazines, comic books, and hard-to-find first editions. We are a Long Island institution, a community hangout, and an experience you can't download.

Why Shop At An Independent Bookstore?

Here's a few great reasons: tax dollars stay local, we bring authors to town and plan fun events, and we are a great place to meet new people at our book clubs! We get to know you and can offer customized book selections.
